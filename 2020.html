Virtual MuniHac 2020

This year, we are planning MuniHac to be a one- or two-day online event filled with Haskell talks and workshops that will be streamed via the internet. MuniHac will be free to attend.

Programme

MuniHac 2020 will take place remotely on Friday, September 11 and Saturday, September 12, 2020. We will open on Friday 11:30 CEST for two days and three tracks of Haskell-related workshops and talks. See the list of confirmed speakers! And if your're here just to connect with other Haskellers or hack on projects, we'll have you covered as well.
